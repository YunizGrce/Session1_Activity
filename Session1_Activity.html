Our Asymptotic Love Story

This heart wrecking story is a prequel to the infamous novel I Love You Since 1892 which is already a heartbreaking story

If you know math you can already forsee how painful the story could be just from it's title that mentions asymptotic, and an asympotote is said to be lines that could grow very close to one another, but they can never get together.

Fun Fact: an asymptote is a horizontal, vertical, or slanting line, its equation is of the form x = a, y = a, or y = ax + b. Also, we can find vertical and horizontal asymptotes of a asymptote function f(x) = (nx2 + nx) / (x2 + x)

Hell University

This mysterious story is about a female lead who got trapped ina school in the middle of nowehere with her friends where they are not allowed to leave, and they are forced to follow the rules of the school.

The Bad Boy's Girl

This story follows a typical love story between a nerdy girl who likes a guy but ends up getting entagled with his hotter bad boy brother.

My Husband Is A Mafia Boss

This funny, action packed, heartwarming and unique story follows a love story between the childish female lead and the mafia boss.
